About me.

Learn more about me.

I am a designer and developer who enjoys clean and fun designs that brings smiles to people’s faces.

My first encounter with designing and coding was in primary school, when I taught myself HTML and CSS to build blog skins as hobby. The hobby lasted for a few years, and I became fascinated with beautiful web designs ever since then. When designing, I love to explore new forms of interaction, and I keep the user in mind, always.

I am a Computer Science undergrad in National University of Singapore.

In NUS, I am an active student who joined various faculty committees. I was an Assistant Student Relations Secretariat in the 19th Computing Club Management Committee, a Float Designer in the Computing RAG Float Committee, and also a Programmes and Logistics Member in the Computing Freshmen Orientation Week Committee.

I am interested in UI/UX Design and I wish to improve myself in this field.

Other than designing blog skins as a hobby in the past, I do not have much real experience in this UI/UX Design. In school, I am currently reading a module CS3240 Design Interaction which I hope can enhance my ability to design meaningful products for users.
